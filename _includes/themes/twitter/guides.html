OpenStack Guides

The following guides discuss downloading, installing, configuring and maintaining OpenStack deployments. In most cases, these guides assume you are using Ubuntu 12.04LTS (Precise) as your base hypervisor system. OpenStack is known to work on other platforms, but we've found Ubuntu's packaging is the most stable to date. If you'd like to see additional content not covered here, feel free to contact us.
