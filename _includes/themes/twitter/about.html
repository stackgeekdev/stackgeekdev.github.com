Company

StackGeek is a Bay Area startup focused on the OpenStack private cloud project. StackGeek consultants are experts at building scalable private and hybrid cloud solutions for companies looking to maximize their infrastructure spend. StackGeek specializes in virtualization of purchased or leased equipment, networking and load balancer configuration, large scale storage solutions, and a variety of custom cloud infrastructure optimization.

If you are interested in cloud technologies, or have a hankering for helping others configure, setup and deploy their own cloud infrastructure, drop us a line. We'd love to hear from you.
